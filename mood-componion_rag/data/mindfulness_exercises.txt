1. **Breathing Exercise**: Inhale deeply for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, exhale slowly for 8 seconds. Repeat 5 times.
2. **Body Scan**: Sit or lie comfortably. Close your eyes and bring attention to each part of your body, from head to toe, observing any sensations without judgment.
3. **Gratitude Journal**: Write down three things you're grateful for each day.
4. **5-4-3-2-1 Grounding**: A grounding technique to help you stay present. Name 5 things you see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you hear, 2 things you can smell, 1 thing you can taste.
